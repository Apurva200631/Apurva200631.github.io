History of Solapur

Solapur has a rich historical background and has been ruled by several dynasties over the centuries. The city was once an important center during the Bahmani Sultanate period.

Solapur Fort, also known as Bhuikot Fort, is one of the most important historical monuments in the city. The fort reflects the military architecture of medieval India.

Over time, Solapur developed into an important textile hub famous for Solapur chaddars and cotton towels.
