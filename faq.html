Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Solapur?

October to February is the best time because the weather is pleasant.

Which temple is most famous in Solapur?

Siddheshwar Temple is one of the most famous temples in Solapur.

How far is Pandharpur from Solapur?

Pandharpur is about 70 km from Solapur.

What food is famous in Solapur?

Jowar Bhakri, Misal Pav, Shenga Chutney, and Puran Poli are popular foods.
