Festivals and Events in Solapur

Siddheshwar Yatra

One of the biggest festivals in Solapur celebrated every year in January. Thousands of devotees visit Siddheshwar Temple during this time.

Ashadhi Wari

A famous pilgrimage where devotees travel to Pandharpur while singing devotional songs.

Navratri Festival

Tulja Bhavani Temple in Tuljapur attracts thousands of devotees during the Navratri festival.
